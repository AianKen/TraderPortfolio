Robert Trade

Founder & Principal Trader

Robert began trading equity index futures from a university dorm room in 2010, blew up two accounts before turning twenty-three, and spent the following decade rebuilding his process from the ground up. He now runs Trade Capital as a single-operator desk — proprietary capital, no outside investors, no AUM-based incentives to manage.

His approach blends discretionary macro reads with systematic execution: positions are sized mechanically, but the decision to engage a market at all remains entirely human. He keeps a public trade journal because, in his words, "a thesis you're willing to publish is a thesis you've actually tested."
